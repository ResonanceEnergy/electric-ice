# ARCHITECTURE — ELECTRIC ICE

## Purpose
Electric Ice business platform — likely an EV-adjacent or clean energy consumer product company under Resonance Energy.

## System Overview
```
[Product Catalog] --> [E-Commerce]    --> [Fulfillment]
[Customer Data]   --> [CRM/Marketing] --> [Analytics]
```

## Components
- **Product Catalog**: SKU management, descriptions, pricing
- **E-Commerce**: Cart, checkout, payment processing
- **Fulfillment**: Order management, shipping integration
- **CRM**: Customer profiles, purchase history, segmentation
- **Marketing Automation**: Email/SMS campaigns, retargeting

## Data Flow
Visitor → Product Browse → Cart → Purchase → Fulfillment → Post-Sale

## Integration Points
- Resonance Energy parent brand
- NCL analytics
- Matrix Monitor (sales dashboard)

## Key Decisions
- Shopify or headless commerce for speed to market
- Agent-managed product descriptions and SEO
